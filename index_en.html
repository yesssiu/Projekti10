Worry-free play with the LittlePlanet

LittlePlanet is Finland's first toy brand whose products are made from plastic waste collected from the sea. With us, you can find a comprehensive selection of toys for children of different ages that fit a minimalist and earthy interior style. You can also bring discarded toys to us for recycling. For us, the three most important values ​​are ethics, ecology, and aesthetics.
