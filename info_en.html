Our story

LittlePlanet started from a shared passion for the sea and nature. In addition to ethics, important values ​​for us are ecology, aesthetics and ease. All our products are carefully thought out in terms of both design and materials.

Our goal is to offer high-quality and aesthetically beautiful toys that fit the interior and are pleasing to the visual eye. There are many kinds of toys available on the market, but their colour schemes and designs are often very colourful. That's why we wanted to design something where the colour scheme is neutral and close to nature.

We founded the LittlePlanet recycled toy brand because we noticed that there are hardly any toys made of recycled material on the market, and especially not companies where it would be possible to take old toys to be easily recycled.

All our toys are made from recycled material and are designed in Finland. Some of the products have been made in cooperation with well-known Finnish designers.
